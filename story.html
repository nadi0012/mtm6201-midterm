About Us

Started out of my home three years ago when I went on maternity leave. Grew from catering to friends and family to craft sales and community events. Through word of mouth, I became more popular but am still just catering for events such as birthdays and weddings on weekends mostly. All my flavors and names on moods which makes my shop unique. I always keep in mind nutrition and make healthier options for health concise individuals.
